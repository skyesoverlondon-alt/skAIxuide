KAIXU
Built for Brutal Reality
Security Posture & Governance
"Demo-grade" apps die the first time billing disputes show up, caps get hit, or a team shares keys in the wild. Kaixu platforms are designed around the failure modes: governance at the boundary, logs that explain why something was blocked, and pricing that can be re-computed later from raw facts.

Central Gateway Contract

No direct provider calls inside apps. Every AI model call is routed, logged, and governed through a single contract point with full audit visibility.

Policy Templates

Team, Agency, and Enterprise policy layers. Owner vs viewer separation with minimal RBAC that scales without becoming a security engineering project.

Device Binding & Per-Key Caps

Device limits, rate caps, model restrictions, and key rotation by default. When something gets blocked — the "why" is in the logs, not a mystery.

Indisputable Invoices

Store raw usage events, compute invoices from a versioned pricing table. If someone disputes a charge, you can re-derive every line item from timestamped facts.

Signed Audit Trails

Every usage event is signed and stored. Full reconstruction of who called what, when, why it was allowed or blocked, and what it cost — at any point in time.

Clean Admin/User Separation

Least privilege everywhere. Transparent audit trails. Operator-grade software that doesn't turn customers into security engineers.
