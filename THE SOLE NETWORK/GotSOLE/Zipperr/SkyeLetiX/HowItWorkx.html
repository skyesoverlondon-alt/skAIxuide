League Rules

The Ground Rules

📏

Height Requirement

All players must be under 6'0" (72 inches) barefoot. Height is verified at check-in before your first game. No exceptions.

👥

Roster Size

Teams have a maximum of 15 players. Minimum 8 paid players required to field a team. All players must complete registration and waiver.

🚫

No Free Rides

Every player on the roster must be paid and roster-active. No bench-only spots. If you're on the roster, you're in the game rotation.

🤝

Code of Conduct

Professional conduct required at all times. Violence, harassment, or unsportsmanlike behavior results in immediate removal without refund.

📸

Media Rights

By participating, you grant media release rights. Games are filmed, highlights are created, and content is used for promotion. Your game gets seen.

💰

Refund Policy

Fees are non-refundable except for documented medical emergencies or league cancellation. Partial refunds at management discretion.
