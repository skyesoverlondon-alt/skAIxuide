SKYELETES TEAM OWNER AGREEMENT

By submitting this application, I agree to the following terms:

1. OWNER RESPONSIBILITIES:

  • Actively recruit players to fill my roster (minimum 8 players to compete)
  • Ensure all rostered players complete registration and payment
  • Attend or arrange coverage for all scheduled games
  • Maintain professional conduct and sportsmanship
  • Communicate schedule changes with at least 48 hours notice

2. COMPENSATION STRUCTURE:

  • $50 bounty for each player who registers using my tracking code
  • 15% commission on ticket sales attributed to my promotional efforts
  • $500 bonus if my team wins the State Championship
  • Payouts processed within 14 days of season completion

3. NO UPFRONT COST: There is no fee to become a Team Owner. Your compensation comes from player bounties and commissions.

4. ROSTER REQUIREMENTS:

  • Minimum 8 paid players to field a team
  • Maximum 15 players per roster
  • All players must meet height requirement (under 6'0")
  • All players must complete waiver and payment

5. MEDIA & BRANDING: I grant SkyeLetes permission to use my team's name, logo, and imagery for promotional purposes. I understand uniform designs are provided by SkyeLetes during the Brand-Lock session.

6. CODE OF CONDUCT: I agree to maintain professional conduct. Violations including violence, harassment, or unsportsmanlike behavior may result in removal from the Owner program without compensation.

7. TERMINATION: Either party may terminate this agreement with 14 days written notice. Earned commissions will be paid for completed registrations.
